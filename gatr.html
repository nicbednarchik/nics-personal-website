ABOUT THIS PROJECT

GATR is a local Seattle punk rock band made up of three best friends from college. As the creative director and designer for the band, I developed the bands full visual identity. For GATR'S first merch drop I created the custom concert poster, branded stickers, and apparel.

What I did: Branding, design, merch mockups + outsourcing, and creative consulting.

Tools & Skills: I'm highly experienced with Canva and used it to design GATR’s full visual strategy and identity.

Inspiration: I drew inspiration from punk rock culture, and vintage band posters from bands like Nirvana and Pink Floyd. My goal was to capture the rebellious energy of the genre and turn it into something bold, nostalgic, and true to GATR's spirit.

What I Leant: This project taught me how to build and manage a visual brand from the ground up, from concept to production. It sharpened my creative problem-solving, attention to detail, and ability to take an idea and turn it into a tangible product. These are the same skills I now apply to software engineering and product management. I focus on thinking through user needs, iterating on design, and delivering clean, intentional outcomes.
