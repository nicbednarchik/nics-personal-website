What is Dibs?

Dibs is an iOS app for browsing your friends’ closets and calling "dibs" on the pieces you love. No payments, no pressure, just curated pieces you already love, from the people you love.

Features

Post your closet items

Call dibs on cute finds

Chat & Coordinate Clothing Swaps

In App Preview

Behind the App

Dibs is my passion project! I wanted a low stress way for friends to swap clothes without turning it into a full blown resale marketplace. No prices, no pressure, just a fun way to call dibs on pieces you already love from the closets of those you already trust.

What I did: I designed and developed the app using Swift and SwiftUI. I’m building out core features like user profiles, closet posting, and real-time notifications. I'm also leading product decisions, user testing, and feature prioritization.

Tools & Skills: Swift, SwiftUI, Xcode, Figma, and Notion (for roadmap + sprint planning).

What I'm Learning: Dibs has been my real life crash course in software engineering and product management. It's taught me how to think like a builder AND a user. Dibs has tought me how to balance clean code with thoughtful user flows, and learning to prioritize the features that matter most.
